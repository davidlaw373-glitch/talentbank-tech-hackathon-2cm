# 建议最终页面数量

| 模块 | 页面数 |
| --- | --- |
| Authentication | 1 |
| Candidate | 10 |
| Employer | 7 |
| University | 6 |
| Shared | 3 |
| **Total** | **27 Pages** |

其中若项目时间有限，建议 MVP 优先开发：

1. Authentication
2. Candidate Dashboard
3. Candidate Profile
4. Job Discovery
5. Job Details
6. Job Application
7. Employer Dashboard
8. Company Profile
9. Job Management
10. Candidate Management
11. Interview Management
12. University Dashboard
13. Graduate Management
14. Notification Center

共约 14 个核心页面即可完成完整系统 Demonstration。
